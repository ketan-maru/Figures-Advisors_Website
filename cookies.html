What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, to remember your preferences, and to provide website owners with analytical information about how their site is used.

Similar technologies include web beacons (small transparent images embedded in web pages or emails), pixels, local storage, and session storage objects. Where this Cookie Notice refers to "cookies," it includes these similar technologies as well.

How We Use Cookies

Figures Advisors uses cookies on figuresglobal.com for limited purposes described below. We do not use cookies to track your behavior across unrelated third-party websites or to build advertising profiles.

Strictly Necessary Cookies Always Active

These cookies are essential for the Site to function properly. They enable core functionality such as page navigation, form submission, and security features. The Site cannot function properly without them and they cannot be disabled.

Examples: session identifiers, CSRF protection tokens, load-balancing cookies.

Analytics Cookies Optional

These cookies allow us to count visits and measure traffic sources so we can understand how visitors interact with our Site and improve its performance. All data collected is aggregated and anonymized — it does not identify you personally.

Examples: Google Analytics (_ga, _gid), page view counters, referral source tracking.

Functional / Preference Cookies Optional

These cookies remember choices you make (such as contact form field entries) to provide a more personalized experience. They do not track your browsing activity across other websites.

Examples: form input memory, user interface preference settings.

Third-Party Cookies and Services

Some pages on our Site may embed content or tools from third-party service providers, which may set their own cookies. We have no control over these cookies. Key third-party services we use may include:

  • Google Analytics — web analytics service provided by Google LLC. Google may use collected data in accordance with its own privacy policy. You can opt out via Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • FormSubmit or equivalent form processors — used to deliver contact form submissions to us. These services may set session cookies to prevent duplicate submissions.
  • Google Fonts — typography loaded from Google's servers, which may log technical information about your device. We use display=swap to minimize data transfer.

We take care to engage only service providers with appropriate data processing agreements and privacy commitments. However, please review the privacy policies of these third parties if you have questions about their data practices.

Managing and Disabling Cookies

You have several options for managing cookies:

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can set your browser to refuse all cookies, to alert you when a cookie is set, or to delete cookies already stored. Common browsers and their cookie management links:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our Site, including contact form submissions.

Do Not Track

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal. Our Site does not currently respond to DNT signals, as there is no universally accepted standard for how websites should respond to such signals.

California residents: California law requires us to disclose whether we honor Do Not Track signals. We currently do not alter our data collection practices in response to DNT browser settings. We do not sell personal information and do not use cookies for cross-context behavioral advertising.
